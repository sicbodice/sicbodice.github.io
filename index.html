Sic Bo Dice Gambling

Keywords：Blockchain, Smart Contract, Gambling

Summary

Sic Bo is a gambling game based on dice rolling. All the gamblers bet with the dealers. Sic Bo is a game in which the bankers always have the upper hand. Because the gamblers can never increase their chances to win by using any technical methods, in the long run, the dealers wins. Relative to the dealers’s long-term interests, the gamblers’ ten-time, even hundred-time, rewards gained by only one bet are rather attractive as well.

Different from the traditional gambling game where the casino plays dealer, Sic Bo uses the smart contract by BSC, which is a set of commitment in digital form. Player of Sic Bo can choose freely to be gamblers or dealers, or both at the same time.

Sic Bo betting contracts are fully open-source and viewable on the blockchain. All data is public, and viewable at bscscan.com. Smart contracts for BNB bets are fully automated and fair.

Wins Payment

The program will automatically do the liquidation work after the bet is taken. For the gamblers who lose, their BNB will become dealers’ profit; for the gamblers who win, the system will reward them according to preset odds. Liquidation time varies according to the size of BNB Smart chain, from 10 to 30 seconds.

If a gambler wins, the compensation according to the contract is at most 1% of the dealer’s account balance. For example, if the dealer has 5000 BNB, the gambler’s bet on any triple would rather not be higher than 1.667(5000x1%x3.33%) BNB, for he would not get more even if he take more bet.

Edge

The dealers have different kind of advantages according to gamblers’ different bets. Take “triple” as an example. The odds are 1:30. The dealer has 13.89% edge. In other words, the expectation of gambler on each triple bet is -0.1389. The gambler loses 0.1389 on each 1 BNB bet. The expectation of dealer on each triple bet is 0.1389. The dealer gains 0.1389 on each 1 BNB bet.

The dealer’s edge is the dealer’s extra gain and the gain will increase as the number of gamblers increases.

Rights

Although the expectation is above zero, the odds are high. The dealer must be wealthier than the gambler. Individual dealer may be weak in power, so Sic Bo has contract for dealer’s rights.

Dealer’s right is to distribute one dealer’s right to all the players who have the certificate of dealer’s rights. These players take turns to be dealer. In specific form, it is the SBD token players hold.Dealer’s rights varies between circulating rights and hold right.

Hold Rights

The rights by contract is hold rights. Hold rights is only for distribution and will not take part in dividend calculation when the dealer redeems his rights and interests.

Circulating Rights

The rights, which is not by contract, is circulating rights. Circulating rights can be traded for BNB at any time. Circulating rights circulate among players.

The upper limit for dealer’s rights is 21 million. It will not increase.

SBD Unit Price

Unit price for dealer’s right is BNB and the price fluctuates. The fluctuation is not caused by buying and selling dealer’s right, for doing so will not cause price change. Gamblers take part in the game, which results in profit, causing the price for dealer’s right to change. In the long run, the money invested will get a profit at least 2.78%, because the dealer’s edge is 2.78%.

Invest SBD

Anyone can invest in the dealer’s rights and gain dividend according to the amount of deal’s rights he holds. When all the hold rights are distributed, you can gain your holding of SBD by buying from the trading market, finding a trustworthy seller or through the exchange office. SBD is a token under BSC BEP20 standard. The address for SBD token is: 0x19e7e376e7c213b7e7e7e46cc70a5dd086daff2a at bscscan.com.

Player can trade for dealer’s right on Sic Bo Dapp, or transfer BNB into the contract and the contract will distribute SBD accordingly. Dealers can trade their SBD for BNB at any time. When you trade for BNB, the contract will deduct some SBD. The contract will ask for permission to deduct a small amount of SBD, just as credit card will ask for merchant’s permission. SBD is private property and its transfer needs permission. The fee for transferring SBD into BNB is 1%.
